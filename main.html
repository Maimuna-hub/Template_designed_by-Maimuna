Best Places To Visit In BANGLADESH

Saint Martins

St. Martin's Island is a small island in the northeastern part of the Bay of Bengal, about 9 km south of the tip of the Cox's Bazar-Teknaf peninsula, and forming the southernmost part of Bangladesh.There is a small adjoining island that is separated at high tide, called Chera Dwip.

Nilachol

Nilachal Tourist Spot is Most Eye catching Tourist Spots of Bangladesh. Situated in Bandarban. It will Take only 30 Minutes to reach Nilachal Tourist Spot from Bandarban Proper.Nilachal is the nearest one of the beautiful tourist spot from Bandarban. It is situated at Tigerpara.

Rangamati

Known as the ‘Lake City’ of Bangladesh, Rangamati is full of matchless beauty and is one of the most visited destinations of Bangladesh.It is located about 77 km away from Chittagong.This district famous for the sky touching the mountain,Water Lake and hanging pool.
